Who am I?

Let's start with a little about me.

A soon to be graduate of Code Institute's Diploma in Web Development, (Level 5), with a background in teaching and education, I bring a different perspective to creating digital solutions. My experience in education has fueled my passion for leveraging web technologies to make learning more effective, engaging, and inclusive. I believe that technology can bridge gaps in accessibility and engagement, empowering learners of all backgrounds and abilities to do their best.

Throughout my transition into web development, I have worked to learn and develop skills in front-end and back-end technologies, applying them to projects that reflect my commitment to creativity and the needs and behaviours of users. My approach emphasizes designing tools and platforms that not only function seamlessly but also captivate users through intuitive interfaces and thoughtful inclusivity.

As a new (close to) graduate, I am eager to collaborate on projects that merge my developing technical expertise with my dedication and passion to education, through creating interactive learning experiences, improving accessibility and inclusivity through the development of innovative approaches to scaffolding of online learning. With a teacher’s insight and a developer’s skill set, I aim to contribute to meaningful innovations in web development that engage, educate, entertain and empower.
